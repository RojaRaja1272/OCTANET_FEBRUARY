FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can I book a ride for someone else using my Rola Cab account?

    Yes, you can book a ride for someone else using your Rola Cab account. Simply input their pickup location and details when making the booking.

  • How does Rola Cab ensure passenger safety?

    Rola Cab prioritizes passenger safety through rigorous driver screening, real-time ride tracking, and 24/7 customer support. Additionally, we provide features such as driver identification and trip sharing for added security.

  • What should I do if I have a problem during my ride?

    If you encounter any issues during your ride, such as a problem with the vehicle or driver behavior, please contact our customer support immediately through the app. We take all complaints seriously and will investigate promptly.

  • What types of vehicles does Rola Cab offer?

    We offer a variety of vehicle options to suit your needs, including standard sedans, SUVs for larger groups or luggage, and luxury vehicles for special occasions.

  • Are Rola Cab drivers licensed and vetted?

    Yes, all Rola Cab drivers undergo thorough background checks and are required to have valid licenses and insurance coverage.
